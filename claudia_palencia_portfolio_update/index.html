Hi! I'm Claudia!

I'm a marketer turned software engineer! I'm known to camp out in coffee shops when I'm not lifting heavy things at the gym...I'm also a mother to a tiny human and have always had a passion for all things tech. I strongly believe that technology means empowerment and hope that by sharing my story, it helps other women continue to pursue their dreams.

Throughout my web development journey I've learned to fail fast and fail often. That's the secret to learning to code. Stepping outside your comfort zone, trying things out until they work, and not being afraid to be wrong or reach out for help are an essential part of programming. Never be afraid of change, failure, or making mistakes....all these are only helping make you a stronger developer.
